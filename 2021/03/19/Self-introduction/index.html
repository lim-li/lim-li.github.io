In the four years of undergraduate, through systematic and comprehensive study, I have a certain foundation in computer hardware and software. In my spare time, I will also brush some algorithm problems on LeetCode, and browse some interesting open source on github. Meanwhile,I have developed my social communication skills and teamwork leadership by taking part in some student union affairs and volunteer work.

I deeply feel the undergraduate knowledge is insufficient, so I choose to continue my studies, study deeper professional issues, and explore my own greater potential, so that I can make more contributions to society and achieve a better life in the future. Hope that through my efforts I can get this opportunity, thank you all teachers!
